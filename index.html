Our Story

A Swedish coffee cottage on Forest Blvd.

Kaffe Stuga means "coffee cottage" — and for half a century that's exactly what it's been: a warm little kitchen in Harris where the coffee's always on and the food is cooked the way it was at home.

Generous plates, fair prices, and regulars who've been coming back for decades — especially the Sunday-after-church crowd. Come early; the good stuff goes fast.
